Packages and Pricing


Alright. Confession time. This page title is very misleading. I don't offer packages and I don't list pricing. Stick with me I have good reason. Quick story: I gave a speech at a high school that serves primarily impoverished kids. The speech was basically how to inspire girls into STEM. One question a parent asked me is "my daughter is bad at math, she's scared she won't be able to make it." I encouraged the parent to tell her daughter not to stress about math, its all about practice. I brought up tutoring and the parent said "the school offers additional help but I can't afford to hire someone." I kept my composure until I got in my car. Then I got really sad.

The private tutoring companies I have worked for charged between $55-$85 / hour. One of them charged students by the minute. True story. I reject the idea that personalized tutoring is only for the privileged. Especially now with all of the uncertainty around school.

So here is how this will work. Lets talk. Message me and tell me what you need. Tough class? Lets try to meet once a week. Just need an intense study session for a test? Lets hit the books! Need something in between? Lets work that out. This is why I don't want to offer packages. There is no one size fits all.

Pricing


For now I am offering a pay as you go / pay what you can situation. Availability is going to be limited as I have a day job and a pretty busy family life. It will also be first come first serve. No worries on if "pay what you can" means $1 or $2 dollars. The only time I will charge a minimum is if I have to travel, and right now that is not even an option.
